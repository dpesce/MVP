Megamaser VLBI Project (MVP)

The MVP aims to realize very-long-baseline interferometric (VLBI) observations of (sub)millimeter water megamasers residing in the accretion disks around active galactic nuclei (AGN). The first stage of the MVP involves identifying which (sub)millimeter transitions exhibit maser activity. To this end, we have been awarded ALMA time (Project 2024.1.00211.S) to carry out a spectral sweep of the Circinus Galaxy across the full set of ALMA Bands 1-10 (covering a frequency range of ~35-950 GHz).

This page provides a current snapshot of the project status.
